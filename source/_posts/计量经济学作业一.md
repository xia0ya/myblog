$$
证：∑\hat{p}_i = ∑y_i，其中\hat{p}_i = \frac{e^{x_iβ}}{1+e^{x_iβ}}。\\
有L(β) = ∑[y_i \ln G(x_iβ) + (1-y_i) \ln (1-G(x_iβ))]\\
= (∑[y_i \ln \frac{e^{x_iβ}}{1+e^{x_iβ}} + (1-y_i) \ln \frac{1}{1+e^{x_iβ}}])\\
= (∑\{y_i (\ln e^{x_iβ} - \ln (1+e^{x_iβ}) + (1-y_i)(\ln (1+e^{x_iβ}))\})\\
= (∑\{y_i x_iβ - y_i \ln (1+e^{x_iβ}) - \ln (1+e^{x_iβ}) + y_i \ln (1+e^{x_iβ})\})\\
= (∑(y_i x_iβ - \ln (1+e^{x_iβ})))\\
又(\frac{∂L(β)}{∂β} = ∑\{y_i x_i' - \frac{1}{1+e^{x_iβ}} \cdot e^{x_iβ} \cdot x_i'\})\\
= (∑{y_i x_i' - \hat{p}_i x_i'})\\
= (∑(y_i - \hat{p}_i) x_i' = 0)\\
即 (∑y_i = ∑\hat{p}_i)\\
$$
